About Louis

Louis is an orange tabby. He is almost 9 years old. That make him a senior cat.

Louis used to have a different home in Pahrump, NV, but his humans couldn't take care of him anymore. A rescue called Foreclosed Upon Pets Inc (FUPI) decided to help him find a new home.

He spent about 7 months with FUPI in Pahrump. He didn't find his new humans there, so he was moved to Las Vegas to give him a better chance of being adopted.

In Las Vegas, he lived at a PetSmart store for about a month with a lot of other cats looking for humans.

One night, two humans came in to say hello to the cats. There were kittens and young cats talking and playing, and they were fun to hang out with.

But there was one cat in a cage on the bottom who was curled up in a ball asleep.

The nice humans noticed that he was a lot older than the other cats, which means that he was going to have a harder time finding his forever home. He seemed sort of sad. They decided that they would take him home and become his humans and make him happy again.

Now Louis lives in a big house with lots of toys. He has a sister named Phoebe to play with. He loves to take naps and sit on his window seat, looking at cars and people outside.
